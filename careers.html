Our culture

Our team of self-starters work remotely with presence in Boston, Silicon Valley, The Washington D.C. Metro Area and Kyiv.

Be humble

  • You are willing to listen and learn from others
  • You constantly try to improve yourself and the company
  • You admit mistakes

Be relevant

  • You communicate openly and provide honest feedback
  • You are non-political

Be tenacious

  • You strive to ensure we remain relevant to customers
  • You ensure your skills remain relevant
  • You build for the long-term success of business

Be candid

  • You care about the customer and empathize with their security, financial, and other needs
  • You gain the trust of customers without instilling fear

Be empathetic

  • You are resilient in the face of adversity
  • You go beyond symptom identification to determine root causes
  • You learn rapidly and fail fast

Be selfless

  • You seek what is best for the company not what is best for yourself
  • You help your team and colleagues
